Why Use Systems Design Tools?

Game systems design requires careful balancing of multiple variables such as damage scaling, cooldown timing, player stats, and multiplayer fairness. These tools allow developers to:

  • Test ability scaling curves across levels
  • Balance cooldown systems for fairness and pacing
  • Simulate PvP encounters and multiplayer scenarios
  • Optimize stat distribution for different character builds
  • Prevent overpowered or underwhelming mechanics
